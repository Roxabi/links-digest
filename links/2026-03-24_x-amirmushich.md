2. THE ACTOR (creating characters)

For commercial campaigns, you need custom avatars that work consistently across any scene.

Images with people increase business metrics:
⤴️ 3x better ER (engagement rate) in Social Media
⤴️ 60% better eye-tracking
⤴️ 30%+ better click-through rate (CTR) in ads

So, let’s train the AI system with a custom Character Kit.  
AI can’t guess what your model’s shoes look like from behind.  
Let’s define it.

MY WORKFLOW:

1️⃣ REFERENCE HUNT:

Savee.it or Pinterest: Filter by high-end editorial boards
Models.com: For clean casting shots
Vogue Runway (www.vogue.com/fashion-shows): For high-end styling

🔎 WORDS TO SEARCH:
- high-fashion street casting polaroid;
- athletic sportswear editorial model full body;
- minimalist activewear turnaround;
- cinematic character portrait raw lighting.

2️⃣ LLM PROMPTING:

Let Claude do the heavy lifting & describe the model properly.  
▶️ Step 1: Upload your chosen image to Claude 
▶️ Step 2: Paste this prompt & run the model:

▶️ Step 3: You’ll get back the detailed prompt for your image generation.  Copy it & go to: https://amirmushich.link/OA-NB2

3️⃣ GENERATION:

Take the prompt and paste it into Google Nano Banana.  To build a robust model, generate 4 variations. 
  
Replace the [FRAMING & ANGLE] variable in your prompt with these 4 views:

1. Full Body shot, frontal view;
2. Bust Up Close-up portrait;
3. Strict Profile view;
4. From Behind (back view).

4️⃣ CHARACTER CREATION:

Let’s bring the talent to life inside the OpenArt ecosystem:

- Go to OpenArt Characters (https://amirmushich.link/OA-Model)
- Upload your set of 4 images.
- The AI will synthesize them into a character ready to be added into your Environments.
- Once created, give your character a name and add into your prompts & Worlds just by tagging it with “@”
