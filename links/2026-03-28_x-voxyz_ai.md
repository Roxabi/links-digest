you installed openclaw and used it to chat.

here's what it can actually do. work to life, all covered.

— work —

social media ops: agent auto-generates topics, creates content, schedules posts. you just review for 30 minutes before publishing. used to post once a day, now five. coverage goes through the roof.

competitive research: give the agent a list of competitors, it auto-scrapes their sites, socials, user reviews, product updates. used to take two days per competitor, now four hours for a full report.

knowledge base: pull all your scattered docs, SOPs, meeting notes into one place, auto-categorized and tagged. new hires stop chasing senior employees around, they just ask the agent.

workflow automation: 8am auto-scans your inbox sorted by priority. 6pm auto-generates daily report draft. friday auto-compiles weekly report with charts included. saves 2 hours of grunt work every day.

customer onboarding: someone pays at 3am, agent sends a welcome message instantly. you wake up to a summary, not a pile of unread messages.

brand monitoring: watches your brand keywords 24/7 across platforms, auto-sorts positive vs negative, pushes critical stuff within 15 minutes. no more finding out about overnight PR fires when you get to work.

github monitoring: auto-tracks target repos for stars, issues, PRs, releases. first to know when competitors ship or the community flags something.

revenue tracking: every stripe transaction auto-summarized, daily and weekly reports generated. no more logging into dashboards manually to check numbers.

— life —

second brain: toss articles, ideas, notes at your agent anytime. auto-tagged, summarized, archived. over time it becomes a searchable knowledge base you can talk to. finding stuff goes from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.

health system: connect apple watch data to your agent. heart rate, sleep, exercise auto-analyzed. flags anomalies early, generates weekly health reports.

side project autopilot: topic research, content drafts, performance reviews all automated. you bring the brain, the agent brings the labor.

life admin: bills, subscriptions, expiring IDs, packages. agent reminds you on schedule, notifies early based on urgency.

family coordination: unified calendar for everyone. auto-detects scheduling conflicts. shopping lists auto-compiled, pushed before you leave the house.

travel planning: give it a destination and dates, get a full itinerary. routes, timing, accommodation, all in one go.

schedule management: calendar, meetings, todos in one place. no more jumping between five apps to figure out your day.
