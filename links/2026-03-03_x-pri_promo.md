3.5M views 🤯
Here's the simple strategy behind my viral launch video for @heylemon_ai :

(AIDA: Attention →  Interest →  Desire →  Action)

1 - Establish the problem you're solving

2 - Introduce solution

3 - Show use cases. NOT features. 

4 - End with call-to-action WITH an incentive.
