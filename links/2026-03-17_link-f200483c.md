There is a hidden industry behind every frontier model you use. When Claude gets better at coding, when GPT gets better at math — someone had to build the training environments that made those improvements possible. A handful of small, mostly stealth companies operate on contracts with the big labs. The RL Environment = task + scorer combination (like a gym for the model). Verifiers must be non-reward-hackable. Distributed model: bounties to thousands of domain experts, automated verification funnel (Tier 1 structural, Tier 2 LLM adversarial, Tier 3 human review) could reduce cost per environment from $750-1500 to ~$117. Someone is going to build this platform.
