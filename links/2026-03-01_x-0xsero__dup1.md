My engagement rate is extremely high, my impressions went from 3M over 9 months to 12M in 3 months 

Here’s what I changed: 

1. Posting much more often 
2. Joined a bunch of communities on X
3. Started making deep dives sharing info
4. Stopped engaging (mostly) in politics
5. I pin posts 2x a day 
6. I reply to my posts with new posts 
7. I quote tweet things that interest me
8. I color match my profile pic w banner
9. I try and share everything that I would find interesting
