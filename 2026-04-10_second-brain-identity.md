**Summary:** Julian Alvarez argues that most AI second brains are missing the *personal context* that makes AI responses genuinely useful. His fix: three core folders.

### The Three Folders

| Folder | Files | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 📁 Identity | `SOUL.md` | Who you are |
| 📁 Aspirations | `GOALS.md`, `HABITS.md`, `12 FAVORITE PROBLEMS.md` | Who you're becoming |
| 📁 Live Logs | `DECISIONS LOG.md`, `LEARNINGS LOG.md`, `EMOTIONS LOG.md` | Ongoing memory record |

### The Glue — `AGENTS.md`
- Describes all documents to the AI
- Instructs the AI on *how* to use and *auto-update* them

### Why It Matters
Without this, AI only knows *what you're working on*. With it, AI knows:
- Your values + identity
- Your goals + blockers
- Your recent decisions + emotional state

→ AI can surface patterns you'd miss
→ AI can act as a coach, not just a tool
→ Second brain → **Life OS**
